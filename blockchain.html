What is
Blockchain Technology?

Blockchain is a secure digital system for storing information. Instead of keeping data in one place, it is shared across many computers around the world.

How Does Blockchain Work?

Think of blockchain like a digital notebook.

📄

1. Block

Every page in the notebook is called a Block.

➜
💾

2. Store Information

Each block stores transactions or important records.

➜
🔗

3. Chain

Every block connects to the previous block, creating a Blockchain.
